Ethical Concerns of AI and Social Media

Source 1

URL https://www.weforum.org/agenda/2020/01/ai-ethics-social-media-responsibility/
Author World Economic Forum
Abstract This article explores ethical challenges posed by AI integration in social media, including privacy violations, biased content delivery, and manipulation of user behavior. It emphasizes the importance of transparent AI systems and responsible platform policies. The discussion highlights the balance needed between technological innovation and protecting user rights to ensure ethical digital experiences.

Source 2

URL https://www.brookings.edu/research/the-ethics-of-artificial-intelligence-in-social-media/
Author Brookings Institution
Abstract This resource examines ethical concerns surrounding AI in social media, including algorithmic bias, data privacy, and misinformation propagation. It discusses how AI can unintentionally reinforce social inequalities and manipulate user choices. The article also proposes strategies for ethical AI deployment, such as fairness, accountability, and transparency, to mitigate potential harm while leveraging technological benefits.
