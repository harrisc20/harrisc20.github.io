Psychological Impacts of Social Media and AI

Source 1

URL https://www.apa.org/news/press/releases/2020/10/social-media-mental-health
Author American Psychological Association
Abstract This article examines the effects of social media on mental health, highlighting risks such as anxiety, depression, and social comparison. It discusses how platforms utilize algorithms to maximize engagement, which can unintentionally amplify negative content and affect user well-being. Recommendations for responsible use and strategies to mitigate negative impacts are also provided.

Source 2

URL https://www.health.harvard.edu/blog/social-media-and-mental-health-2021051822605
Author Harvard Health Publishing
Abstract This resource explores the connection between social media use and mental health outcomes. It explains how constant exposure to curated content can lead to negative social comparisons, lower self-esteem, and addictive behaviors. The article emphasizes the importance of moderation and mindful engagement to protect psychological well-being in the digital age.
